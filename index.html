How it work ?

1. Booking:

First, users must access the app or website of their choice and register their accounts on the platform. After accessing the profile through the login process, one can browse the different available specialities. Then, they must choose the speciality for the primary issue they need consultation for and select an available doctor.


2. Discussion:

After completing the payment process for the doctor, the client has to wait for some time, around 60 seconds on average, for the doctor to reach out. Then, the professional asks for the medical reports and symptoms that the patient is facing. In some of the apps, the video consultation feature is available. You
can avail of that if necessary.
Based on the symptoms the patient mentions, the doctor will take some time to create a prescription as per the diagnosis. They will provide additional tips and even allow a few days of free re-consultation support. This second appointment will allow the health professional to see how the treatment works.
In case the patient has seen no major changes, the doctors make corrections to the prescription or assign more medication to the patient.


3. Medication:

Through some of these apps or website, you can directly buy the medicines that are available in the prescription from the app. Download the prescription for purchasing them at offline pharmacy stores, if needed.
So, as you can see, the process is fairly straightforward. All you need is a stable Internet connection and you can connect with a medical expert from anywhere in the world.
